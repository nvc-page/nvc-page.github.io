Planting trees is one of the easiest and most
sustainable ways to positively affect the
environment. We need trees now more than
ever!. Communities across the state have been
devastated with the loss oftens of millions of
ash trees since the emerald ash
borer was first discovered in
metro Detroit in 2002. Trees are
our lifeline to cleaner air and
a healthier environment.

Trees improve air quality by producing oxygen.
They also store carbon, offsetting harmful byproducts of
fossil-fuel burning, and also moderate the effects of sun and wind, and they clean the air by trapping
dust, pollen and other pollutants.
